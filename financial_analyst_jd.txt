Financial Analyst - Investment Banking

Position: Financial Analyst
Department: Investment Banking
Location: New York, NY

Job Description:

We are seeking a highly motivated Financial Analyst to join our investment banking team. The ideal candidate will perform financial analysis, build financial models, and support M&A transactions.

Required Qualifications:

• Bachelor's degree in Finance, Accounting, Economics, or related field
• Minimum 3 years of experience in financial analysis or investment banking
• Strong proficiency in Excel and financial modeling
• Experience with financial statement analysis
• Knowledge of valuation methodologies (DCF, comparable company analysis)
• Excellent analytical and quantitative skills
• Strong attention to detail

Preferred Qualifications:

• MBA or CFA certification
• Experience with Bloomberg Terminal
• SQL or Python programming skills
• Experience with Tableau or Power BI
• Prior investment banking experience
• Advanced Excel skills (VBA, macros)
• Experience with M&A transactions
• Strong presentation skills

Key Responsibilities:

• Build and maintain complex financial models
• Perform company and industry research
• Prepare pitch books and client presentations
• Analyze financial statements and create valuation models
• Support deal execution and due diligence processes
• Create financial forecasts and projections
• Collaborate with senior bankers on client engagements

Experience Level: 3-5 years preferred
